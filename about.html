Who We Are

M. Yakub, Abdijabar & Associates CPA(K) is a dynamic professional accounting and advisory firm founded by CPA Mohamed Yakub and CPA Abdijabar Khalif. The firm is built on strong ethical values, technical excellence, and a deep understanding of the Kenyan business and regulatory environment. Both partners are graduates of the University of Nairobi and registered members of the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (ICPAK), each bringing over five years of extensive hands-on experience in accounting, audit, tax, and advisory services.
